About Me

Hi,I am Mohd Faizan, the founder and CEO of The NGO ,The Power of One, working to improve lives through programs focused on food distribution, healthcare, education, and supporting beggars in need. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Integral University Lucknow, I aim to combine my knowledge with my passion for social welfare. Based in district Sitapur, I started this NGO to address critical issues and bring positive change to society. With a belief in the power of small, sincere efforts, I strive to create a future filled with hope and compassion. My ultimate goal is to inspire others to join this mission and make a lasting impact.

Our Aim

Our mission is to provide food, education, and medical aid to those who need it most. By empowering underprivileged families, we aim to create opportunities for sustainable growth and a better tomorrow for everyone. Every step we take is a step closer to making a significant impact on society

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child has access to education, every family has food on their table, and no one is denied medical care due to financial constraints. Together, we can make this vision a reality and uplift countless live.
